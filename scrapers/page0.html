Keep your money safe. Your security is our priority
Remember
We'll never contact you to ask you:
  • To confirm log on or personal details.
  • To transfer money.
  • For credit or debit card details by email.
  • To hand over your card except in a branch.

Suspect calls
We'll never contact you to ask you:
  • For your PIN or Online login credentials.
  • To verify your login credentials.
  • If you are unsure of who you're speaking to hang up and call the bank on a different line if possible.
  • Or go into a branch.

Policy:
You are a valued customer and we want to ensure you keep your money safe. For more information on how to do this and for full details on our security policy see our online guide.
